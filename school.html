Our Story

A Legacy of Learning & Leadership

RBT Vidalaya was established in 1995 with a singular vision: to provide world-class education rooted in Indian values. For nearly three decades, we have shaped thousands of leaders, innovators, and responsible citizens.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower every student with knowledge, skills, and values to thrive in a dynamic world — academically, personally, and ethically.

👁️

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted learning institution — recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and holistic student development.

⭐

Our Values

Integrity · Respect · Excellence · Innovation · Community Service — the pillars that define the RBT way of life.

Admissions 2025–26

Follow these simple steps to secure your child's place at RBT Vidalaya. Limited seats available — apply early!

1

Submit Inquiry Form

Fill the online form with your child's details and preferred class. Our team will contact you within 24 hours.

2

Document Verification

Submit previous school records, birth certificate, Aadhar, and passport-size photos for verification.

3

Entrance Assessment

A simple aptitude test and interaction session for Classes VI and above to assess learning levels.

4

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Complete fee payment online or at the school office and receive your enrollment confirmation instantly.
